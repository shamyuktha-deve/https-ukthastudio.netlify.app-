Who I Am

Hi, I'm Shamyuktha 👋

I'm a freelance web designer and developer based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. I specialize in building clean, responsive websites that actually help businesses get more customers online.

My journey started when I realized that most small businesses in India don't have a proper online presence — and that's a huge missed opportunity. So I decided to bridge that gap by offering affordable, high-quality digital services.

I love the intersection of design and code. Whether it's building a website from scratch, designing a poster that stops the scroll, or editing a video reel that goes viral — I bring creativity and precision to every project.
